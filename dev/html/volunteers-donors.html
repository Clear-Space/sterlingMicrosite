Recognizing Cumulative Contributors

The Sterling Hall School gratefully acknowledges the significant financial contributions of Sterling Hall community members whose cumulative giving in support of the school’s vision, mission and priorities is $25,000 or more. All gifts, including annual giving, major and leadership giving, realized planned giving, and receipted gifts-in-kind, contribute to the cumulative total. Collectively, these gifts have tremendously enhanced the educational experience for Sterling Hall boys. Thank you to these donors for their long term financial support and commitment to Sterling Hall.
