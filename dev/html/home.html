Together for a Bold Future

A Message from Doug

Chair, Board of Governors

"Together we're setting Sterling Hall's course for a bold future."

2013 – 2014 was another exciting year of accomplishment at Sterling Hall. We continued to progress across our programs, people and facilities. In particular, several important new academic initiatives were successfully rolled-out, as Rick Parsons has detailed in letters to the school community. Rick and all of the faculty and staff at SHS again demonstrated, day-in and day-out, that they are an extremely committed, capable and caring team. Our boys are being guided well.

Our Year in Review

Rick Parsons, Principal

2013-2014 was a year of inspiration and aspiration, fuelled both by renowned visitors to the school and by our own remarkable community. With the support of our dedicated families, faculty, staff and students, the year culminated in a bold vision for Sterling Hall and a truly exciting direction for our future. In September, we were enthralled with the talent and joy of the Kenyan Boys Choir, whose international acclaim includes performing at President Barack Obama’s inauguration.
