Our Dedicated Board Members

Our Board of Governors is a dedicated group of volunteers who bring extensive and diverse professional expertise to the leadership of The Sterling Hall School. The Board provides strategic and policy direction for the school and has a fiduciary duty to protect the school’s assets, property and long- term viability.

The Board of Governors, consisting primarily of SHS parents and past par- ents, works closely with Principal Rick Parsons in determining and evaluating the strategic direction of the school. The Principal, together with the faculty and staff, is then responsible for implementing the strategy and man- aging the day-to-day operations of the school.

Board members meet regularly along with the Principal, Director of Finance, Executive Director of Advancement and Parents Association President. In addition, the Board operates through Standing Committees of Governance, Strategic Planning, Property and Finance, as well as ad hoc committees when needed.

We are very fortunate to have a Board comprised of talented and dedicated Governors who work diligently to ensure a bold future for our school.
