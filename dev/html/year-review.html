Our Year in Review

A Message from Rick

Principal

2013-2014 was a year of inspiration and aspiration, fuelled both by renowned visitors to the school and by our own remarkable community. With the support of our dedicated families, faculty, staff and students, the year culminated in a bold vision for Sterling Hall and a truly exciting direction for our future. In September, we were enthralled with the talent and joy of the Kenyan Boys Choir, whose international acclaim includes performing at President Barack Obama’s inauguration.

Our Board of Governors is a dedicated group of volunteers who bring extensive and diverse professional expertise to the leadership of The Sterling Hall School. The Board provides strategic and policy direction for the school and has a fiduciary duty to protect the school’s assets, property and long-term viability.

The Board of Governors, consisting primarily of SHS parents and past parents, works closely with Principal Rick Parsons in determining and evaluating the strategic direction of the school. The Principal, together with the faculty and staff, is then responsible for implementing the strategy and managing the day-to-day operations of the school.

Board members meet regularly along with the Principal, Director of Finance, Executive Director of Advancement and Parents Association President. In addition, the Board operates through Standing Committees of Governance, Strategic Planning, Property and Finance, as well as ad hoc committees when needed.

We are very fortunate to have a Board comprised of talented and dedicated Governors who work diligently to ensure a bold future for our school.

Rick Parsons
Principal

It is truly remarkable and impressive that 100% of our faculty and staff supported SHS with a gift this year.
